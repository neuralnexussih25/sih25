A golden chariot drawn by swans carries the infant Pandu to heaven. Yama, grim-faced, explains his human fate: marriage to twin sisters, progeny, and eventual death for slaying them in anger. Duryodhana gathers his vast army near Indraprastha palace.
He confronts Bhima amidst feigned ignorance about the Kauravas' true resentment towards their cousins' prosperity. The battlefield at Kurukshetra lies strewn with carnage after Arjuna's devastating volley of arrows leaves Drona critically wounded, his pupil forever changed by the war's brutal reality.
